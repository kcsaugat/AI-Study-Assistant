## How Your Friend Uses It (Step-by-Step)

Send them this guide:

### First Time Setup (2 minutes)

1. **Click the link**: https://ai-study-assistant.vercel.app
2. **Sign up**: Click "Register" and enter:
   - Email address
   - Password (at least 6 characters)
3. **Create first note**: 
   - Click "New Note"
   - Paste your study material
   - Click "Save"

### Features They Can Use

#### 📝 Summarize Notes
1. Go to "Notes"
2. Click a note
3. Click "Summarize"
4. AI generates a summary in seconds

#### 📊 Generate Quiz
1. Click "Generate Quiz"
2. Answer multiple-choice questions
3. See your score
4. Review answers

#### 🎯 Create Flashcards
1. Click "Generate Flashcards"
2. AI creates flashcard decks
3. Study cards (flip to see answers)
4. Track progress

#### 🤖 Chat with AI Tutor
1. Go to "Chat"
2. Type your question
3. AI responds instantly
4. Full conversation history saved

#### 🌙 Dark Mode
- Click moon icon in top-right
- App switches to dark theme
- Great for night studying
